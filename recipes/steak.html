Steak

This is a meal for meat lovers!

This steak is buttery, juicy, and perfect for data night. Let's get to cooking!

Ingredients

  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 (1 1/2-pound) flank steak
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. season beef
  2. heat and oil pan
  3. cook for 2.5 minutes on each side
  4. let rest for 5-10 minutes
